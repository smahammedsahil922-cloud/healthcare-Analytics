What it does

Built for real insight

Automated pipeline that reads raw CSV data, runs analysis, and outputs a fully formatted, professional Excel report — no manual work needed.

📊

KPI Dashboard

Total revenue, cost, profit, average age, and readmission rate in one executive summary sheet.

🏥

Department Analysis

Compare Cardiology, Neurology, Pediatrics, Orthopedics, and General — by patients, cost, and profit.

💳

Insurance Breakdown

Medicare, Medicaid, Private, and Self-pay — average costs, revenue, and readmission rates per type.

🦠

Disease Insights

All 5 diseases ranked by patient volume with average treatment cost and revenue figures.

📈

Monthly Trends

17-month revenue and cost trend analysis with profit/loss colour-coding per period.

⚙️

Fully Automated

One command — python healthcare_analysis.py — generates the complete report instantly.
